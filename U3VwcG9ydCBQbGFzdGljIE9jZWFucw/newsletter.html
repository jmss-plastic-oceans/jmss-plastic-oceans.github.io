Submission

We want to hear from you!

Please email us at poastudents.australia@gmail.com if you would like to:

    • Submit an article relating to the issues that Plastic Oceans Australasia addresses
      Get involved through presenting a simple idea/pitch
      Contact the John Monash Science School Media Team for further publications of our current newsletters and articles


    • Guidelines for Submission Emails

    When sending an email you will need to include:

    • Your Name
      Year level
      School
      A short (less than 100) description of your submission of article or idea/pitch
      Attachments of articles must be in pdf format

    • Our People

    Plastic Oceans at JMSS is a diverse group made out of almost 60 people from different grades around the school.
